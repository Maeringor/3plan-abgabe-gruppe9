Questions you might have

Are the apps free to use?

All three apps are free to use as of now. There will be no guarantee for updates or expansions that will be available in the future, but there won't be any hidden costs.

Do I have to pay any additional fees?

Since all apps are completely free to use we like to keep our offer simple and don't charge any extra fees.

Why do I need 3 apps?

We could have combined all three apps in one, but created an ui that is easy to understand and work with. Every app serves a different purpose with different use cases.

Do I have to add all my personal information?

By default, you just have to submit your email adress and name, but if you like to interact with other people and want them to learn more about you, you can share addtitional information about you like your country and address.

How do I add friends?

If you open your friend list, you will see a field to add a friend, where you can search for your friends.

Is my data safe?

By default you won't be asked to share any more personal information than necessary. But if you choose to, you can feel safe about your personal information, just like all the in-app information like events and reminders.

How many people can I invite to events?

To every event and project created, you can add an unlimited amount of people for every purpose.
